Dark Chocolate Milk Shake

It is simple to make and always taste good. Even without ice cubes and ice cream, chocolate milk shake taste good .

Ingredients:

  • 5-6 pieces - Dark chocolate (75% cocoa)
  • 1 cup - chilled cream milk
  • 3 tbs - cream
  • 1 tbs - dairy milk spready(optional)
  • 1.5 tsp - powder sugar (according to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Take 3 tbsp of milk in a small microwave safe bowl. Add dark chocolate pieces.
  2. Microwave high for 20 seconds. Stir well. Let it melt down completely.
  3. Add melted dark chocolate to blender. Add chilled milk with 3 tbsp cream. Add powder sugar and dairy milk spread.
  4. Blend it well until frothy. Decorate the glass with chocolate syrup.
  5. Pour the milkshake into a glass and garnish with grated chocolate and serve.
