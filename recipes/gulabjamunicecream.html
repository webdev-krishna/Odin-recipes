GULABJAMUN ICECREAM

This is an Indian Sub-continent Delicious Sweet Called "GulabJamun Icecream"

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 tin Nestle MILKMAID
  • 2 cups (200 gm) Flour
  • 600 gm grated Paneer
  • 200 gm Sooji/Rava
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • as per requirement Oil for frying
  • 2 Ltrs water
  • 1 kg Sugar
  • 6-8 nos, coarsely crushed Cardamom

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire; add cardamom and keep aside to cool.In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestle MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough, do not knead too much. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  2. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup, once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame. Serve warm.
